About Us

A local team you can trust.

Total Care Deep Cleaning was started in 2019 by a small team of local professionals from Udupi who wanted to bring proper, reliable cleaning services to families and offices in the region. Before we started, most people had no option but to rely on part-time maids or overpriced agencies from bigger cities.

Today we serve hundreds of homes, apartments, and offices across Udupi, Manipal, Karkala, Kundapur and nearby areas. Our team uses professional-grade equipment — steam cleaners, HEPA vacuum machines, and eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for your family and pets.

We are not a big company. We are a small local team that takes pride in doing our work properly. Every job is supervised personally, and we don't leave until you are satisfied with the result.
