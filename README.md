## 📋 About

**Total Care Deep Cleaning** is a landing page website for a professional cleaning service business operating in the coastal Karnataka region — specifically in **Udupi**, **Manipal**, and **Karkala**. The website is designed as a mobile-first, single-page application to help customers learn about services, view pricing, and easily get in touch via phone or WhatsApp.

Trusted by **500+ families** in coastal Karnataka, Total Care Deep Cleaning offers honest pricing and trained staff for all types of cleaning needs.
